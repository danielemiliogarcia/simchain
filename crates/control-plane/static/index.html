    Confirm faucet transaction
    
      
    
      
    This creates a real regtest transaction with a 0 sat fee. Simchain submits it
        directly to both miners with a local 100 BTC virtual priority delta so their next
        block includes it. The virtual amount is not paid or transferred. This keeps
        faucet transactions quick and models a dark-pool/direct-to-miner path, where
        transactions with different public fee rates can still be included when they
        reach a miner directly and match that miner's policy.
